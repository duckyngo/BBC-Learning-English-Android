 <ul>
  <li>Could you say that again please?</li>
  <li>You're going a bit too fast for me.</li>
  <li>Have you any guidance about how to take notes?</li>
 </ul>
 <p>And here are Professor Not's note taking tips...</p>
 <ul>
  <li>Write down the important bits… I'll emphasise these bits in the lecture.</li>
  <li>Write down what I say in your own words – although if I quote someone, make sure you write it down word-for-word.</li>
  <li>Use words, abbreviations, symbols or even pictures to summarise what I say. If you miss something, leave a gap in your notes, and get the information from me later.</li>
  <li>Remember to read through your notes after the lecture.</li>
 </ul>"
